Bride and Groom

  • How We Met
  • Who We Are

We met at work in the fall of 2013. We had both joined Appian that summer and we were taking our first professional steps in the software industry. That winter saw us both working on demanding projects that required late hours. Our late night coding sessions together plus a healthy dose of Google GChat led to more frequent greetings in the hall.

About this time some colleagues of ours arranged a weekend ski trip in West Virginia and we decided to drive the ten hour winding trip through the mountains with each other. At this point we were probably either going to kill each other or fall in love. Fortunately the latter occurred and as soon as we got back to Virginia we planned our first real date.

We had dinner and followed this with a middle school-esque hang out at the Tysons Corner mall. We walked around and talked for nearly two hours and then relocated to a nearby bar after the mall closed, because neither of us wanted the night to end. About a week later we made it official and not long after that we realized that nothing could be better than spending the rest of our lives together.



Mandy is a 23 year old software consultant. She grew up across several continents but calls Frederick, Maryland her home. She makes a mean cocktail, has a degree in bioengineering, and will talk to pretty much anybody about anything.

David is also a software consultant. He is 27, has a master's degree in public policy, and is proud of his Texas roots. You can find him on Saturdays rooting for Baylor football.





The Wedding Party



















April 26th



Ceremony

The ceremony will start at 11am on Sunday, April 26th, 2015. It is scheduled to take place outside, in the lovely pavilion behind the Ceresville Mansion. If the weather is not amenable to an outdoor ceremony, then the event will be moved to inside the mansion. The average temperature for late April is around 60° and the dress code is semi-formal so please keep this in mind when planning your attire. There is ample parking on-site located very close to the ceremony location. A cocktail hour in the front parlor of the mansion will immediately follow the ceremony.



Reception

The reception will feature a brunch theme, breakfast cocktails (as well as wine and beer), full entrees and an omelet bar. In keeping with the theme, the traditional cake will be replaced with doughnuts! Be prepared for an afternoon of fabulous food and drinks, non-stop dancing and music, and overwhelming amounts of love. A DJ will keep the party moving until 3pm.
